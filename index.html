About Me

Hello! I'm Jaimin Patel, a passionate mathematician and educator. My journey in the world of mathematics began with a fascination for patterns and logical reasoning that has only grown stronger over time.

I specialize in Number Theory, Mathematical Analysis, and Applied Mathematics, with a particular interest in the applications of mathematics to real-world problems and computational methods.

Through this website, I aim to share my knowledge, resources, and passion for mathematics with students, educators, and fellow math enthusiasts. Mathematics is not just a subject—it's a way of thinking and understanding the world around us.

My educational background includes a Ph.D. in Mathematics with a focus on Number Theory and Cryptography.
