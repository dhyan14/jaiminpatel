About Me

Hello! I'm Jaimin Patel, a passionate mathematician and educator with over 10 years of experience in teaching mathematics at various levels.

I specialize in Complex Analysis, Differential Equations, and Linear Algebra, with a particular interest in applications of mathematics in engineering and computer science.

Through this website, I aim to share my knowledge, resources, and passion for mathematics with students, educators, and fellow math enthusiasts.

Teaching Philosophy

Conceptual Understanding

I believe in building strong conceptual foundations rather than memorizing formulas. Understanding the 'why' behind mathematical concepts is crucial.

Personalized Approach

Every student learns differently. I adapt my teaching methods to accommodate various learning styles and paces.

Problem-Based Learning

Mathematics is best learned through problem-solving. I encourage students to tackle challenging problems that stimulate critical thinking.
